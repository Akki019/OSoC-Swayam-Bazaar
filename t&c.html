Return & Refund Policy

At SWAYAM BAZAAR, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not happy with your order, you may return eligible items following the guidelines outlined below:

  1. Eligible Items for Return:
    • Perishable goods, such as fresh produce, dairy, and frozen items, are eligible for return if they do not meet the quality standards or if you can genuinely prove issues related to bad hygiene.
    • Non-perishable items must be returned in their original unopened packaging to be eligible for a return.
  2. Return Timeframe:
    • Perishable goods:You must initiate the return process within 2 hours of the delivery time.
    • After 2 hours, we are unable to process returns or offer refunds for perishable items.
    • Non perishable goods:You must initiate the return process within 7 days of the delivery time.
    • After 7 days, we are unable to process returns or offer refunds.
  3. Return Process:
    • To initiate a return, please contact us within the specified timeframe.
    • Provide your order number and details of the items you wish to return.
    • Our pick-up team will inspect them to ensure they meet the return policy criteria.
    • If the items are eligible for a return, we will process your refund within 5 business days.
  4. Receiving Damaged or Incorrect Items:
    • If you receive damaged items or products that do not match your order, please contact us immediately.
    • Provide details and images of the issue, and we will arrange for a replacement or refund as applicable.
  5. Non-Returnable Items:
    • Items that have been opened, used, or are not in their original condition are not eligible for return.
  6. Cancellation Policy:
    • You may cancel your order before it has been shipped.
    • If the order has already been shipped, please follow the return policy guidelines.
